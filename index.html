The Thesis

AI won't save
bad data.

Every company is talking about AI. But most are building on foundations that will fail them — siloed data, undefined metrics, no data culture, and no strategy for what AI is even supposed to do.

The AI winners of the next decade aren't going to be the companies that deployed the most models. They'll be the ones who treated data as a strategic asset first.

That's the shift I help companies make — from AI hype to AI leverage.

"The best AI strategy is almost always a better data strategy in disguise."
